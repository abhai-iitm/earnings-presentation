Risk Assessment & Capital Adequacy

Value at Risk (VaR) Calculation

Our risk management framework utilizes advanced statistical models:

Portfolio VaR (95% confidence level):

$$VaR_{95\%} = \mu - 1.645 \times \sigma \times \sqrt{t}$$

Sharpe Ratio for Portfolio Optimization:

$$Sharpe = \frac{R_p - R_f}{\sigma_p}$$

Current portfolio Sharpe ratio: 1.34

Capital Adequacy Ratio:

$$CAR = \frac{Tier\ 1\ +\ Tier\ 2\ Capital}{Risk\ Weighted\ Assets} = \frac{\$45.2M}{\$312.1M} = 14.5\%$$

Our CAR of 14.5% is well above regulatory minimums of 8%. The VaR model shows our 95% confidence daily loss exposure is $2.1M. The Sharpe ratio improvement indicates better risk-adjusted returns compared to last quarter's 1.18.
